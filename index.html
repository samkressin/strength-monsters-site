My Story — Why This Exists

Scroll the feed. Turn the dial. Open your screen — and the noise comes pouring in. An entire world Built for Binge, not memory. A thousand voices, all shouting at once. Smash that like button. Forget why you clicked. Two sides of the same drama coin, flipped endlessly to keep your eyes on the screen. ” Every click a distraction. Every post another sedative.

Somewhere beneath that flood, I’m still drawing. Still working. Still here. Pushing back with ink and motion — trying to break through the static.

I’ve screen printed my own merch, hauled it to conventions, drew these stories by hand and kept it moving - with zero studio backing.

I started Strength Monsters in the margins — literally.

Getting up at 4 am drawing before driving my kids to school, building this universe one page at a time.

I just wanted to make something real — weird, loud, handmade.

A universe full of characters that felt alive.

Then it clicked;

If I could animate it I could show people the world instead of telling them about it.

So I scraped together what I had put together my own little team of independent artists, and brought Strength Monsters to life.
